TrueStory 1

Even by Delhi's miserable January standards, it was a bitterly cold night when we met Chandan in 2013. Volunteers with the Precious Madad Foundation were distributing blankets to the homeless sleeping outside AIIMS. We saw Chandan, who was then 12 years old, on the pavement, covered with few blankets, moaning in pain. He was with his parents who told us that he was suffering from bone cancer and was undergoing chemotherapy at AIIMS. When they ran out of cash, the family, which had travelled to Delhi from Bihar, moved to the pavement.

Our Mission

We are a non-profit organization on a mission to bring Good Food, Education, Health, Business, Marriges, Home, Vehicle and every basic needs to the people across rural India. Join us, to partake in the Precious Madad Foundation. We believe the best way to fulfill our mission is to encourage people across the world to embrace our cause. Precious Madad Foundation is active in more than 10 countries around the globe.

Miscellaneousinfo

The Precious madad Foundation has been established with the objective of providing all the basic facilities related to the bread and cloth houses, education, health and business of the poor disabled helpless.
Through the Foundation, assistance will be provided from time to time to those individuals or children who really need it. The Precious madad Foundation not only helps those who need them, but the Precious madad Foundation takes care of its members equally. If any member needs any kind of financial social help, then the remaining members of the Apostle Help Foundation meet their needs.
